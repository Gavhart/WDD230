Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Kenai Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about the Kenai Chamber of Commerce and the services they provide.

Target Audience

The target audience for this site is the general public, specifically those who are interested in the services provided by the Kenai Chamber of Commerce.

Color Schema

The color schema for this site will be a blue and white color scheme.

Typography

The typography for this site will be a sans-serif font.

Wireframe

The wireframe for this site will be a single column layout with a header, nav, and main section.
