The Kenai River is a famous river located in the Kenai Peninsula of Alaska, United States. It is approximately 82 miles (132 kilometers) long and flows from the Kenai Lake in the Kenai Mountains to the Cook Inlet, which eventually leads to the Gulf of Alaska.

Kenai Boats refers to a type of boat commonly used for fishing and recreational purposes on the Kenai River in Alaska. These boats are specifically designed and built to navigate the river's unique conditions and cater to the needs of anglers and outdoor enthusiasts in the area.
